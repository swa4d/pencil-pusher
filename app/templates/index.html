Overview

The basics of our generator

Our Goal

The Pencil Pusher project was formed from the fact that many people, even writers and artists, often find themselves lacking in ideas in their day-to-day lives. By providing them with free, imaginative short stories created with a prompt and a click of a button, our product floods the user with inspiration, allowing them to get past any mental block.

Technology Used

This product was created using the programming language Python, the transformer GPT-2, as well as datasets that we found on Google and Kaggle. The website was made using HTML and CSS.

Multiple Models

Our team spent time building various AI models that generate short stories based off of six genres: fantasy, horror, fairytale, humor, and science fiction. Each of our models is trained on a variety of databases depending on which genre it is in charge of, all to create amazing short stories for you!
