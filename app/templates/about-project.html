Our Journey

  • #1

    Learning Python:

    Our group began by learning the basics of python and data analysis. We worked to utilize different python libraries to modify texts and data sources to fit our needs.

  • #2

    Studying AI:

    We then moved forwards into studying AI itself and its workings. In this stage we watched many presentations about neural networks and different types of text generation AI's. We researched the model we would eventually use, the GPT-2/Neo transformer.

  • #3

    Data Cleaning:

    Finally, we began to prepare our ten different datasets from various resources for training the models. We were required to modify the file types and filter out useless information that could skew Pencil Pusher's generation.

  • #4

    Training the Models:

    Pencil Pusher was nearing fruition as its five different AIs were trained individuially on different datasets to make up our project's five different genre options. This major step to completion was important and took about a week and quite a bit of patience.

  • #5

    Website:

    With the models trained and our code complete, all that was left was to make the website and present the information well. As you the reader can hopefully see; our website turned out great!

  • #6

    The Future:

    We hope to eventually introduce more genres to Pencil Pusher and help it reach more and more audiences in the future. In addition to this story length could also be improved and expanded, as could the amount of data the GPT-2/Neo models have access to.

What is AI?

AI or Artificial Intelligence is a type of technology that is able to mirror the functions of humans. For example, a machine that is considered an AI, is able to think much like a regular person. They can also be used to make complex decisions, or even write text similar to a person!

What is NLP?

NLP or Natural Language Processing is the ability of a computer program to understand and process human language. Our class had specialized in this, and we had used our knowledge on this to create our models.

What is Python?

Python is a programming language that can be used for a variety of things, such as building websites. We used this to program our models.

What is GPT-2?

GPT-2, which stands for Generative Pre-Trained Transformer 2, is a model that has been trained using 40 GB of data, from the internet. It is used for text prediction, and we had used this to train our models.

What are HTML and CSS?

HTML, or Hypertext Markup Language is a system that is used to structure pages on the internet, as well as the content in those pages. CSS is a system that is used to design a website and is used to change the appearance of a website. In other words, HTML is the base of a website while CSS is used to decorate it.

What Inspired this Project?

Our team was inspired to create this AI by our common problems found in writing. We discovered that we—in addition to many others in the writing community—sometimes suffered from writer's block. We decided to help with that issues by creating Pencil Pusher: The AI Short Story Generator. Our project uses a medley of sources in a variety of genres to allow for the most unique ideas to be presented. From our Sci Fi AI's usage of complex words, to our Fairy Tale AI's understanding of morals, our project helps with word flow and creativity with ease.
